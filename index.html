Restoration and fitting of bespoke joinery


Here at Tuxworth Joinery, we are dedicated to excellence and offer Carpentry, Joinery and Decorating services in Walsall and the surrounding area. We work to the highest standards, ensuring only the very best will do for our customers. Our services cover many different aspects of woodwork including refurbishments, restoration and the supply and fitting of bespoke joinery. We have a wealth of expertise and more than 15 years of experience in the industry. We work across a number of sectors including domestic & residential, retail and boating.

Kitchens

For most people the kitchen is the heart of the home. Our kitchens are unique and completely tailored to your specification. The carcasses are made using birch ply. Birch is known for its strength and durability and performs very well in applications demanding high strength and rigidity. The framing and doors are made of sing tulip wood (poplar) which is renowned for its ability to take paint well, resistant to decay and when sanded, primed and painted will hold up well to normal wear and tear for many interior projects. All timber work is hand painted using little green paint.


Bathrooms

Creating beautiful tailored bathrooms to suit your every requirement using some of the best quality fixtures and making bespoke cabinetry to fit your space perfect, allowing us to completely project manage your bathroom from design through to completion.
